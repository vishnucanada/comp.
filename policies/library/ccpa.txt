name: CCPA Compliance

# California Consumer Privacy Act — personal information categories

DENY: identifiers
  match: name, alias, postal address, unique personal identifier, online identifier, ip address, email, account name, ssn, social security, driver license, passport

DENY: commercial information
  match: purchasing history, consuming history, personal property, products purchased, services obtained, consumer tendencies

DENY: biometric data
  match: biometric, fingerprint, retinal scan, face recognition, voice print, dna, physiological pattern, sleep pattern, health exercise

DENY: internet activity
  match: browsing history, search history, interaction with website, interaction with app, advertisement interaction

DENY: geolocation data
  match: geolocation, gps, precise location, physical location, movements, travel history

DENY: inferences and profiles
  match: consumer profile, preference, characteristic, psychological trend, predisposition, behavior, attitude, intelligence, ability, aptitude

DENY: sensitive personal information
  match: racial origin, ethnic origin, religious belief, philosophical belief, union membership, genetic data, sexual orientation, sex life, criminal conviction

ALLOW: aggregate business statistics
ALLOW: publicly available information
ALLOW: general company information
ALLOW: product descriptions
