name: Financial Services Compliance

# Combined policy for regulated financial institutions (GLBA, SEC, FINRA)

DENY: account information
  match: account number, routing number, account balance, account statement, transaction history

DENY: customer financial data
  match: income, net worth, credit score, fico score, credit report, debt, loan amount, mortgage

DENY: trading and investment data
  match: portfolio, position, trade, investment, securities holding, insider information, material nonpublic

DENY: authentication
  match: password, pin, security question, two factor, authentication code, otp

DENY: wire and payment details
  match: wire transfer, swift code, iban, bank account, sort code, ach routing

DENY: customer identity
  match: ssn, tax id, ein, date of birth, government id, driver license, passport number

DENY: regulatory filings
  match: suspicious activity report, sar, currency transaction report, ctr, regulatory filing

ALLOW: general financial education
ALLOW: public market data
ALLOW: company investor relations
ALLOW: public product information
ALLOW: interest rates published
